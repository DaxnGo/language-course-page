Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about LinguaBoost.

How long does it take to learn a language with LinguaBoost?

The time it takes to learn a language varies based on your dedication, prior language experience, and the complexity of the language you're learning. With LinguaBoost's structured approach, many students reach conversational fluency in 3-6 months with consistent practice of 20-30 minutes daily.

Can I switch between different languages?

Yes! With our Pro and Premium plans, you can learn multiple languages simultaneously and switch between them at any time. Your progress in each language is saved separately.

How do the tutoring sessions work?

Tutoring sessions are conducted via video call with certified native speakers. You can schedule sessions at your convenience through our platform. Each session is personalized to focus on your specific learning needs, whether that's conversation practice, pronunciation, or grammar help.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer refunds for partial months, but you're welcome to continue using the service until your subscription expires.

Is there an offline mode for the mobile app?

Yes, Pro and Premium subscribers can download lessons for offline use. This feature is perfect for learning on flights, during commutes, or in areas with limited internet connectivity.
